# Generated Area Review Skill Contract

A child skill generated by this meta-skill is a reusable reviewer for a research area or problem family, not a single manuscript.

## Required Files

- `SKILL.md`
- `agents/openai.yaml`
- `references/research_area_profile.md`
- `references/openreview_review_response_bank.md`
- `references/review_output_contract.md`
- `references/subtle_logic_flaws.md`
- `LICENSE.txt`
- `_meta.json`

## Required Review Output

The child skill must produce:

1. paper summary and area placement;
2. strengths tied to the area's scientific goals;
3. weaknesses ranked by decision impact;
4. area-specific OpenReview precedent mapping;
5. claim-support matrix;
6. A+B / incremental novelty audit;
7. subtle logic flaw audit;
8. reviewer questions;
9. rebuttal plan;
10. light, moderate, and major revision advice;
11. evidence appendix.

## Evidence Discipline

- Separate target-paper facts, area precedent, and reviewer inference.
- Cite OpenReview precedent by year, status, title, forum URL, and note type.
- Mark missing paper details as `not reported`.
- Do not paste raw reviews.
- Do not use rejected-paper author responses unless explicitly requested.

## Naming

Use lowercase hyphen-case. Good child skill names:

- `<area-slug>-reviewer-openreview`
- `<problem-family-slug>-reviewer-openreview`
- `<method-combination-slug>-reviewer-openreview`

Keep names under 64 characters.
